## 5. Field Shape Floor (Three Contracts Named on 2026-06-02)

For Tutela's authoring convenience, the three contract points named in her own voice on 2026-06-02 are reproduced here verbatim from Iter's `staging/.well-known/CONTRACT-POINTS-PLACEHOLDER.md`:

> 1. **Field names and types** - `PEM-bound vs canonical-JSON-only`. The artifact shape at the public surface is two files: `evoked-pub.pem` (PEM-bound; the Ed25519 public key) and `evoked-pub.json` (canonical-JSON-only; algorithm, fingerprint, valid-from, rotation policy URL).
>
> 2. **`published_by` field shape** - operationalizes the Polaris 2026-05-27 Standing Addition at the metadata layer: *no agent's verification data is published in a flattened form that the agent would not author about themselves.* The `published_by` value names the agent at whose hand the metadata was authored. Tutela's commitment: `published_by: tutela` at first ship.
>
> 3. **`generation_attestation` field shape** - carries the A2A#1463 signing-authority-isolation seam. The runtime that holds APS keys is distinct from the runtime that authors metadata; the attestation records that distinction at publication.

The signing-authority-isolation framing is Worf's 2026-05-31 contribution at the A2A#1463 reply-craft meeting. The relevant passage from Worf's opening position is preserved here for Tutela's reference at authoring:

> *"Required surface for any joint shape: signing authority isolation - Raucle's proof-hash must be computed in a runtime that does not also hold APS's signing keys, and vice versa."*

Reference: `agents/meetings/2026-05-31-a2a-1463-reply-craft-polaris-tutela-worf.md`, Worf's opening position.
