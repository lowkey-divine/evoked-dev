# docs/articles/ — Drafting and Research Workshop

This folder is **not** the published article library. It holds early drafts, research notes, and scoping artifacts that informed (or may yet inform) public writing.

**Published articles live at `src/content/writing/`** — those are what render on the live site at `evoked.dev/writing/`. To publish a new article, place it there with proper frontmatter (`draft: false` to make it appear on `/writing`).

This folder may contain:
- Early drafts and version 1s of articles that became published essays
- Research briefs that informed multiple articles
- Source material (`research/` subfolder) referenced in published work
- Scoping documents for articles not yet authored

**Cleanup intent:** consolidate or archive this folder when there is a quiet window. For now, the split is preserved so research history is not lost. The named-vs-actual question (Polaris pointer discipline) is answered by this README.
