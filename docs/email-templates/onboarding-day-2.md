# Onboarding email (Day 2-3 after subscribe)

Drafted 2026-06-18 as the second touch in the welcome sequence. Sent ~48-72 hours after the subscriber receives the immediate welcome email.

**Purpose:** Introduce Evoked's worldview, set the rhythm expectation, give three concrete starting points so the new subscriber has somewhere to land.

**Voice register:** founder-public. Vuong/McEnerney discipline applied. No em-dashes, no sycophancy markers, no AI slop.

**Sender:** Erin (personal, not "team")
**Reply-to:** the inbox you actually read

---

## Suggested subject line

> Three places to start

(Alternates if you want to A/B: *"Where to begin"* / *"For the first week"* / *"What we wanted you to read first"*)

---

## Email body

Glad you're here.

You subscribed in the last couple of days, which means you're already thinking about AI in a way most people aren't. That's worth honoring.

We made you something. Three places to start, depending on what brought you in.

---

**If you want to understand what we're actually doing**

[Fifteen Frameworks, One Missing Layer](https://evoked.dev/writing/fifteen-frameworks)

We mapped the existing AI governance frameworks and named what is not in them. Most teams discover the gap after they have already shipped. The piece shows where the missing layer goes and what it does. Read this first if you want the framing of everything else we publish.

---

**If you want to see what's happening right now**

[We Are the Variable Being Adjusted](https://evoked.dev/writing/we-are-the-variable-being-adjusted)

Two companies, three signals, one week in June 2026. The piece reads what the three architectures show about the AI industry's posture toward users. Your father, your nephew, you - the user is being adjusted by systems that won't say so. This one is recent and concrete.

---

**If you want something to do**

[What Should Your Agent Refuse?](https://evoked.dev/writing/what-should-your-agent-refuse)

The question nobody is asking. The answer changes how you build, how you ship, and how you sleep. Read this if you make decisions about AI systems and want a frame you can take back to your team on Monday.

---

What you can expect from here:

A letter when there is something worth sending. Usually one to two pieces a week. Sometimes silence for longer when the work does not warrant a send. The brand promise is "we evoke. We never extract." That applies to your inbox too.

Reply to this if you want. I read every one.

- Erin

P.S. If you build or manage AI for a living, the Sovereignty Assessment lives at [evoked.dev/products/sovereignty-toolkit](https://evoked.dev/products/sovereignty-toolkit). It is a 47-point structured audit across seven domains. Most consumer AI products score between 11 and 19 out of 47. Knowing where you are is the first audit.
